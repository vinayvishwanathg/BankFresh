Installing the Barclays app on a new device?

If you have a current account with us, you can transfer your app to a new device without registering again.

You’ll need an activation code – you can get one in the app on your old device, if you still have it.

1. Log in to your app on your old device

2. Select ‘More’ from the bottom of the page

3. Select ‘Manage accounts’ and the ‘Generate activation code’.

If you don’t have your old device, you’ll need to download and register for the app on your new device.

We currently don’t support Android (Go edition) devices. If you’re using an Android tablet, you can download the Barclaycard app to see your accounts, even if you don’t have a Barclaycard.
